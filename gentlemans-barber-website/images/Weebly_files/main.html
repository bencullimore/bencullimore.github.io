    • My journey started at 18 with an apprenticeship in hairdressing. With my newly acquired skills it didn't take long before I was cutting my friends hair in my Dad's kitchen.

      It felt a natural move to go down the path of barbering and over 20 years later I'm cutting the same friends hair and they're bringing their sons in for their first cut.

      I think it's safe to say I've found my calling. I feel very lucky to go to work and meet new people, building relationships and friendships. To top it all off I get to open my first barber shop in the area it all started for me .
